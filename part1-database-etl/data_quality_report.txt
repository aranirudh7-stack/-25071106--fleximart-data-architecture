FlexiMart Data Quality Report

1. Missing Values
- Some customers have missing email addresses.
- Some products have missing price values.
- Some transactions have missing customer or product IDs.

2. Duplicate Records
- Duplicate customer records identified and removed during ETL.
- Duplicate transaction IDs handled during transformation.

3. Data Standardization
- Category names standardized (Electronics, Fashion, Groceries).
- Dates converted to ISO format (YYYY-MM-DD).
- Phone numbers normalized to numeric format.

4. Data Validation
- Foreign key consistency ensured.
- Invalid records logged and excluded from loading.
